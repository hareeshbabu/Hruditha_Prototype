RailcarRx

RailcarRx was established by PRIMUS Global Services, a leading developer of software products with numerous years of expertise in customer software development for railcar leasing, railcar repair and management companies. RailcarRx was developed as an innovative, comprehensive, cloud-based solution to manage the critical aspects of operations for repair shops, rail car owners and railroads. RailcarRx was built by subject matter experts with input from leaders in the rail industry whose common goal was to significantly improve throughput and profitability.

RailcarRx was built in 2014 and officially launched in October of the same year. We have successfully grown our customer base and worked to continually improve our system to make it more user-friendly and feature rich than any other system currently in the market.
